• ➡️ 911 Emergency Number:

    If you or someone you know feel an immediate danger, call 911


  • ➡️ 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline Number:

    If you or a loved one is in distress and considering suicide, contact the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline for free and confidential help, information, and resources 24 hours a day, seven days a week. You are not alone yourself.


  • ➡️ YouthLine: (877)968-8491

    Call the YouthLine hotline to receive teen-to-teen crisis support during select hours and help from adults at all other times.


  • ➡️ Crisis Text Line: 741741

    Crisis Counselor are trained volunteers and not professionals. They can provide support, but not medical advice.


  • ➡️ Military Helpline: (888)457-4838

    Call the Military Helpline for 24/7/365 confidential crisis support. Hotline staff are veterans or others trained in military culture who understand what you’re going through.
